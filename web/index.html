What's Inside

Everything your HOA needs,
in one place

From monthly dues to renovation permits — HOA Connect digitizes and streamlines every part of community management.

💳
Payment Management
Homeowners submit payment receipts directly from their phone. Admins review and approve with one click. Full payment history and annual totals always visible.
Receipt Upload Proof Review Payment History
🏊
Amenity Booking
Browse available facilities, check live schedules, and book time slots in seconds. Alarm notifications remind homeowners before their slot begins. Only updated members can book.
Live Availability Slot Booking Booking Alarm
🚗
Vehicle Sticker System
Request annual vehicle stickers from the app. Once approved, a secure QR code is generated that rotates daily — preventing duplication or replay attacks at the gate.
QR Entry Pass Daily Rotation Guard Scanner
🏠
Renovation Permits
Submit renovation permit requests with all required documents and worker information. Admin reviews each file, tags valid/invalid, and updates permit status in real time.
Document Upload Worker Registry Status Tracking
📢
Announcements
Push community-wide announcements that appear as auto-scrolling banners on every homeowner's dashboard. Set expiry dates so outdated notices disappear automatically.
Auto-Scroll Banner Expiry Dates Instant Push
📋
Entry Logging
Guards scan vehicle QR codes using the HOA Connect app. Every scan is timestamped and logged — giving admins a complete, searchable entry history at the gate.
QR Scanning Entry Logs Guard Role
